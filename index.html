Pizza

A classic homemade pizza with a crispy golden crust, rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Simple, delicious, and perfect for any time of the day.

Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Total Time: 35 mins

Servings: 2–3 people

Difficulty: Easy

Cuisine: Italian

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water

For topping

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1–2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Olives (optional)
  • Oregano & chili flakes

Nutrition

Nutrient Amount
Calories 285 kcal
Carbs 36 g
Protein 12 g

Tips:

For a crispier crust, preheat the oven for at least 15 minutes before baking. Add fresh basil after baking for extra flavor.
